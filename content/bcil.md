Shaping the Future of Learning.

We employ a complement to traditional lecture-based learning: students design prototypes, run experiments, and take their ideas from concept to execution. The program offers experiential learning through innovative course offerings, a speaker series, leadership development workshops, and extracurricular opportunities, with programming spanning social entrepreneurship, civic leadership, community engagement, peer programming, and STEM. It's open to all Brentwood students, and participants have launched profitable businesses, earned patents, and published award-winning research.
