# Belldegrun Center for Innovative Leadership

Shaping the future of learning through an innovative program: students design prototypes, run experiments, and take their ideas from concept to execution. The program offers experiential learning through innovative course offerings, a speaker series, leadership development workshops, and extracurricular opportunities, with programming spanning social entrepreneurship, civic leadership, community engagement, peer programming, and STEM. It's open to all Brentwood students, and participants have launched profitable businesses, earned patents, and published award-winning research.

We are expanding into AI and software startups this year! Whether you want to build an app for your clothing brand or the next agentic hedge fund, submit a pitch. Come work with the latest technologies, gain software engineering experience, and learn entreprenuership skills.
